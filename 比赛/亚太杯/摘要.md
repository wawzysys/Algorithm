随着社会进步和经济发展，宠物行业作为新兴产业在全球范围内快速崛起。本文基于附件数据和补充收集的数据，运用多种数据分析和预测方法，对中国宠物产业发展趋势和市场需求进行建模分析。

\begin{abstract}
With social progress and economic development, the pet industry as an emerging sector has risen rapidly worldwide. Based on appendix data and supplementary collected data, this paper employs various data analysis and forecasting methods to model and analyze China's pet industry development trends and market demand.

First, using the \textbf{Entropy Weight Method} and \textbf{Gradient Boosting Decision Tree (GBDT) Model}, we comprehensively evaluated China's pet industry development over the past five years. Through feature importance analysis, we found that single-person household ratio, urbanization rate, and GDP per capita are key factors affecting industry development. The model achieved R² values of 0.8962 and 0.9243 for cat and dog predictions respectively.

Second, we constructed a hybrid model combining \textbf{Multiple Linear Regression} and \textbf{LSTM} to forecast global pet food demand. This hybrid model improved prediction accuracy through weighted fusion, controlling short-term (within 1 year) prediction errors within ±5\%. The forecast shows steady growth in global pet food demand from 2024-2026, expected to exceed 40 million tons by 2026.

Third, we applied \textbf{ARIMA Time Series Model} and \textbf{Holt Linear Trend Method} to predict China's pet food production value and export value. We innovatively combined these two prediction methods and enhanced forecast reliability through cross-validation.

Finally, we established a \textbf{Multidimensional Quantitative Analysis Framework} to assess the impact of international economic policy changes on China's pet food industry, including trade policies, tariff adjustments, and non-tariff measures.

\end{abstract}
